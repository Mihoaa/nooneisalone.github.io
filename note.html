Tips

Thank you so much for volunteering! We want to make sure that you have a good time writing a letter, so here is a guide to help you out. Before your start, read through these tips.

  • Please DO NOT remind them that it is a good time to be with family and friends, as many of them don't have that in their lives.
  • DO NOT share any religious themed subjects
  • Make sure to be kind and encouraging!
  • Do not share any personal information (eg.phone number, email)
  • Please no profanity and keep the subject on appropriate topics
  • Have fun and remember that you are doing a great kindness!

Possible Ideas

If you don't know where to start, here are some ideas that you can write about! Please make sure to

  • Share your favorite moment of your day.
  • Share something that you learned about yourself during isolation
  • Share something good that is going around the world!
  • Share something that you are looking forward to (ie: read a book, learn to cook)
  • Share something new you tried during this quaratine
