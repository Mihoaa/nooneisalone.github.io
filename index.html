No One is Alone

Do small things to be your bigger self

Current Problem

  • The COVID-19 outbreak has left countless people experiencing loneliness more than ever. Anyone can experience loneliness regardless of age, and some are hit with loneliness much more than others.
  • Recent studies have shown that loneliness and isolation can result in high blood pressure, heart disease, obesity, a weakened immune system, cognitive decline, depression, anxiety, and even premature death.
  • Many see their social worlds becoming smaller as their social connections are cut off. This makes the already difficult time even more difficult as social isolation takes a toll on the mental and physical health of countless people.

Our Approach

We offer a way for you to provide support for those that feel isolated by providing a system that connects people and fosters companionship.

You can either write a letter that will be sent directly to those who need it, or we will schedule a one-on-one conversation where you can call someone who needs some comfort. Whatever you choose, your effort will truly make a real difference.
