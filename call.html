Tips

Thank you so much for volunteering! We want to make sure that you and your caller have a good time, so here is a guide to help you out. Before your call, read through these tips.

  • Make sure to call at the scheduled time so you don’t keep them waiting
  • Go to an area where there is not a lot of background noise
  • Try your best not to interrupt, and don’t be afraid to share your thoughts and stories
  • Speak slowly and clearly, especially for the seniors that are hard of hearing. Make sure to check in every now and then to see if they can hear you clearly.
  • Try your best to keep the conversation positive! We want to make sure that both of you have a fun and happy conversation that can lift their spirits!
  • Please no profanity and keep the subject on appropriate topics
  • Keep in mind that they are a completely different person with different opinions and beliefs, so be open-minded and kind!
  • Have fun and remember that you are doing a great kindness!
