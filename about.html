About

Everyone is prone to social isolation and loneliness. We rely on the support of our volunteers to help solve this problem.

Our Mission & Value

No one should feel alone during this difficult time. We should all be there for each other as a caring community.

FAQ

Why should I volunteer?

Right now, people in your community need help more than ever. This is an easy way that you can give back to your community and help those who need it. A friendly conversation or a kind-hearted note during this time goes a long way, and you can make a huge difference in someoneâ€™s life.

What is the time commitment?

Both options have very little time commitment and are very flexible depending on how much time you can put in. There will be a 5 minute check in with our team before you begin making calls, but you only need to do this once. The call will take about 10 minutes, and your frequency of making calls is up to you. Writing a note will also take about 10 minutes, and you can write as many as you would like.

But can't germs be transported via paper?

We will ensure that the proper safety precautions are put in place to sanitize everything before delivery.

Who would benefit from your help?

You would be helping those who are feeling particularly isolated because of the quarantine (the older adults, homeless, tenderloin residents, etc).

For calling a person in need, will we have the same companion every week?

This is subject to change, but most likely you will have the same companion if you plan to do this long term.

Are there any age requirements?

There are no age requirements for making the call or sending notes. But before you make your first call, our team will make sure that you are fit.

How much do these services cost?

It is completely free of charge because we believe that everyone deserves to have someone during this time.

What locations does No One is Alone serve?

We are based in San Francisco but anyone can sign up wherever you are!
